The simple math behind early retirement!

Your savings rate decides how long you need to work for.

The larger the proportion of your income you save, the faster you can reach FIRE! This is due to two effects. Firstly, the more you save the faster your investments will grow. Secondly, the less you spend the less investments you will need to cover your living expenses.

As a rough rule of thumb, every €1 expense per month requires €300 of investments to cover the expense, at a typical 4% investment return. So every euro matters!

Below you will find a chart with a rough guide as to how long FIRE will take depending on the proportion of salary to expenses saved. This is based on a number of assumptions specified in the list below the chart. For a more accurate calculation, please check out the FIRE calculator on this website!


Assumptions of the above graph

  • You can earn 5% post-inflation returns on your investments during your saving years.
  • You live off a 4% safe withdrawal rate once financially independent.
  • As an extra safety margin, you only touch the gains made on investments.
